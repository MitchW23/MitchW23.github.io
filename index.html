My First Guitar

I recently decided to embark on the journey of learning guitar. It is something I have always wanted to do and really just never had the time to do so.
Looking to eventually learn to play rock and metal music, I decided to go with an electric guitar. A prodiment brand that is used for this type of music is Ibanez.
So I decided that was a good starting point. Ended up getting the Ibanez Gio GRX70QA which is a really cheap starting guitar.
I thought this guitar really looked cool and wanted to be able to play on it, and it did not break the bank like most guitars would.
